1. The World Bank data
	country_level_data_0.csv (https://datacatalog.worldbank.org/dataset/what-waste-global-database; data)
	country_level_codebook.csv (https://datacatalog.worldbank.org/dataset/what-waste-global-database; notes about data)

	Contains the following data:

	- Total amoun MSW generated in tonnes/year.
	- Waste composition (glass, metal, plastic, wood, yard garden green waste, food organic waste, paper/cardboard, rubber/leather, other) in percentages.
	- Special waste (agricultural, construction and demolition, e_waste, hazardous waste, industrial waste, medical waste) in tonnes/year.
	- Waste collection coverage by area (urban/rural/total) in percentages of waste/population/households/geographic area of the country. 
	- Waste treatment (anaerobic digestion, compost, recycling, incineration, sanitary landfill with gas system, controlled landfill, landfill unspecified, open dump, waterbody pollution, other, unaccounted for) in percentages.

3. OECD data
	MUNW_17062020003103960.csv (https://stats.oecd.org/Index.aspx?DataSetCode=MUNW)

	Contains the following data:
	- Types of waste disposal (Material recovery (Recycling + Composting), Composting, Recycling, Incineration, Incineration with energy recovery, Incineration without energy recovery, Landfill) in percentages, including percentages of waste disposal and recovery.
	- Waste composition (Waste from households, Household and similar waste, Other municipal waste, Electric and electronic equipment waste (WEEE), Bulky waste) in thousands of tonnes/year.
	- Waste by origin and destination (Municipal waste generated, Municipal waste treated, Amount designated for recovery operation, Amount designated for disposal operations)in thousands of tonnes/year.
	- Waste treatment (Composting, Recycling, Incineration with energy recovery, Incineration without energy recovery, Total Incineration, Other recovery, Landfill, Other disposal) in thousands of tonnes/year.

	FOOD_WASTE_17062020004933853.csv (https://stats.oecd.org/Index.aspx?DataSetCode=FOOD_WASTE)

	Contains the following data:
	- Waste by activity (Households, Agricultural production, Eurostat proxy for manufacturing of food products including tobacco, Eurostat proxy for services) in tonnes/year.
	- Food waste by activity (agriculture, restaurants, grocery stores, house holds, etc.) in tonnes/year.
	- Food waste by activity (agriculture, restaurants, grocery stores, house holds, etc.) in percentages.

	WSECTOR_17062020010507254.csv (https://stats.oecd.org/Index.aspx?DataSetCode=WSECTOR)

	Contains the following data:
	- Generation of waste by sector (agriculture, energy production, manufacturing, mining, etc.) in thousands of tonnes/year.

	TRADEENV_IND12_17062020170243909.csv (https://stats.oecd.org/Index.aspx?DataSetCode=TRADEENV_IND12)

	Contains the following data:
	- The amount of exports and imports of waste and scrap as defined in Kellenberg (2012) in current USD and in kilograms for all countries (not just OECD) between 2003 and 2016. HS code lookup https://www.flexport.com/data/hs-code/391520-waste-paring-and-scrap-of-styrene-polymers



4. Eurostat data (https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/data; in Tables-by-theme/Environment-and-energy/Environment/Waste/Waste-streams) 
	- ten00106.tsv - Generation of waste by economic activity
	- ten00108.tsv - Generation of waste by waste category
	- ten00110.tsv - Waste generated by households by year and waste category
	- ten00062.tsv - Recovery rates for packaging waste
	- ten00063.tsv - Recycling rates for packaging waste
	- t2020_rt110.tsv - Landfill rate of waste excluding major mineral wastes
	- t2020_rt120.tsv - Recycling rate of municipal waste
	- t2020_rt130.tsv - Recycling rate of e-waste
	- sdg_12_50.tsv - Generation of waste excluding major mineral wastes by hazardousness


5. Jambeck et al. 2014 "Plastic waste inputs from land into the ocean" data (https://science.sciencemag.org/content/suppl/2015/02/11/347.6223.768.DC1?_ga=2.59807592.435860622.1590747671-1037101167.1589133553)

	Contains the following data:
	- Waste generation rate in kg/person/day.
	- Percentage of plastic in waste stream.
	- Mismanaged plastic waste generated by coastal population (based on population densities within the 50 km coastal area) in 2012 and 2025 in tonnes/year.
